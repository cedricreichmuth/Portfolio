About Me

My name is Cedric and I was born and raised in rural Switzerland. Throughout all years of my education, I've actually always been the life science kind-of-guy. After I had graduated from High School, however, I took an internship on Multimedia Production. I was working with loads of tools and it just wouldn't let me go how these work. I got a very brief insight on development and it fascinated me! So after my military service, I decided to take my chance and go to Toronto to improve my English and, most importantly, to learn some tech skills. Currently I'm at RED Academy and the rest of the story is yet to be told.
